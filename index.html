    Frequently Asked Questions

    How long does it take to sell my car?

    It only takes 10 minutes to answer the questions /self car evaluation and get a final offer. Once you have accepted the offer, we'll pick your car from your home and transfer the amount to your bank account within 24 hours.

    Does my car stay with me?

    On confirmation of the deal and after money transfer to you, we'll pick your car from your place, until then, it stays with you.

    Do I have to pay anything upfront?

    No, You don't have to pay a penny upfront! We hold a nominal amount as RC hold at the time of buying, but it is entirely refundable once the ownership transfer (RC) is complete.
